How can we Help?

Buy

Have you ever thought of owning a home and didn’t know where to start? No matter your experience or timeframe, we’re here to help. My team and I are available to help you through the home buying process whether it’s your first time buying a home or if you’re a seasoned investor.

Sell

Are you looking to downsize or upgrade the current home you live in now? My team at Scrupps Estates will help you strategize how to sell your home for top dollar all while making sure you have a place to live after you sell.

Invest

Has investing in real estate ever crossed your mind? Let us help guide you through your real estate investments from start to finish by ensuring you are being taken care of by true professionals in the field.

Educate

Does real estate interest you, and you’d like more information on how to get started? We teach real estate classes every month online and are happy to help educate you on the market, your options, what’s available, and the real estate process in general.

What We Do

We pride ourselves in not only being a one stop shop for buying, selling, and investing in real estate; but we also conduct business in a simplistic manner to not overcomplicate the process so you can focus on what’s most important: Finding a home that’s unique to you.

We partner with local companies that we have used ourselves to ensure that you are being taken care of by the best in the business. We are also supported by agents nationwide, so that if your real estate plans take you outside of the state of Washington, you can count on nothing but the same level of professional service. We look forward to connecting and helping you navigate the world of real estate!
